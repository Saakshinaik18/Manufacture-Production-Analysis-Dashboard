This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of manufacturing production and rejection trends across departments, machines, and employees. The objective is to identify production inefficiencies, high rejection sources, and operational bottlenecks to improve overall manufacturing performance.

The dashboard tracks key metrics such as total manufactured quantity, rejected quantity, wastage percentage, machine-level rejection, and employee-wise rejection contributions. Interactive filters for Month, Department, Operation Type, and Delivery Period allow users to drill down into specific operational areas and monitor performance trends.

Used SQL for data extraction and transformation and Power BI for data modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization to identify machine-level, department-level, and employee-level rejection patterns, enabling data-driven decisions to reduce wastage and improve manufacturing quality.

Key visualizations include monthly rejection trends, manufacturing vs rejected quantity comparison, department-wise rejection distribution, and machine-level rejection analysis, enabling stakeholders to quickly identify problem areas and make data-driven operational decisions.
